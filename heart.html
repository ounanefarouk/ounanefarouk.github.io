Interactive Heart's Eye

This interactive visualization features a 3D heart model with an animated eye that follows your cursor. The eye can blink randomly or be manually closed using the controls. The shader creates realistic lighting effects and eye movements.

Interactions:

  • Drag to rotate the heart
  • Move cursor to make the eye follow
  • Use sliders to adjust lighting
  • Buttons toggle eye state and shader effects
